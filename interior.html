DGA recommends putting together a mood board of ideas and brands that you like to help with the budgeting process. The DGA build model helps you to experiment with different finishes and costs, think about smart swaps and actively manage your resources.

Kitchens

Some standard rules of thumb when scoping the kitchen: the entire budget should be no less than 5% of the house value and no more than 10%. Allow 50% of the budget for cabinets, 20% for worktops, 15% for appliances, 15% for labour.

Radiator Choices

  • Pressed Metal Radiators
  • Column Radiators
  • Milano Radiators

Pressed Metal Radiators

Pressed steel radiators are affordable and readily available. Fins can be added to the back for extra heat efficiency.

Column Radiators

Generally traditionally styled radiators made from hollow tubes of mild steel. Often described as ‘cast-iron style’ radiators, column radiators offer the same character and charm of real cast iron but heat up faster.

Milano Radiators

Modern styling and high quality – a radiator to fit every space. Lots of brands available.
