Wall Finish

Very much an area for personal taste and creativity. Here it is important to think practicality about durability and maintenance as well as aesthetics and cost.

Wall Tiling

  • Ceramic
  • Porcelain
  • Large Format
  • Marble
  • Stone

Ceramic

Glazed tiles are durable, decorative and suitable for many settings including kitchens and bathrooms.

Porcelain

Porcelain tiles can look like stone, slate or wood.

Large Format

Large format tiles give the illusion of space; they are made of porcelain.

Marble

Marble is luxurious but requires maintenance and can be costly.

Stone

A natural look which requires upkeep and maintenance.

Wall Painting

  • Dulux Emulsion
  • Farrow and Ball
  • Wallpaper
  • Bespoke

Dulux Emulsion

Standard quality paint. Check out the Dulux expert tips on this link for painted wall finishes.

Farrow and Ball

Heritage paint colours and highly pigmented paint. Beloved of interior designers—representative of boutique paint manufacturers. Interior and exterior options are offered.

Wallpaper

A huge variety of prices, types and designs—good wall finish is needed.

Bespoke

Polished plaster, stucco, specialist paint finishes. Speciality finishes such as polished plaster, liquid metal and hand painted are about making a statement and tend to be used for a feature wall.
